All About Starlings

Starlings are my favorite thing I've made, and I love telling stories about them!

What Exactly Are Starlings?

Starlings are Celestial creatures coming from outer space! They also have many friends like Heartlings, Notelings, and Astrodinos (very creative naming). They have a long story I am very proud to work on with my good friends every day, one day I wish to write a book featuring them.

Starling 101

Starlings come in many shapes and sizes, some gay, some French, some American, and yadadada you get the point. Many Starlings live just like us, working like us, eating like us, and having fun like us! They make very good pets.

Design

Starlings are designed in a simple way that is a pain to draw. Most Starlings are drawn featuring a 5 pointed star design with curved and very round (somewhat fat) edges that appear more as a ink splat than an actual star. Their eyes have slits for eyes and a small curvy smile or frown.
